--- layout: lab-object title: Vortex ---

This tool is used to mix small vials of liquid. It consists of an electric motor with the drive shaft oriented vertically and attached to a cupped rubber piece mounted slightly off center.

Never operate this tool without a shaking head securely attached, wear eye protection, do not use or mix solvents and flammables on or near the mixer, and shake hazardous samples in appropriate containment vessels.
