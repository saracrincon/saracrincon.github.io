--- layout: lab-object title: Shaker Incubator ---

This are often used for cell culturing, cell aeration, and solubility studies. They are used to provide stable temperature conditions and orbital agitation at variable speeds to affect the growth of cell cultures.

This machine operates under high speed - the lid should remain closed while in operation. If the lid unexpectedly opens, wait for the rotation of the machine to stop. Chemical resistant gloves should be worn while handling bio-oil and/or other chemicals.
