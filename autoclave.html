--- layout: lab-object title: Autoclave ---

This is a device that uses steam to sterilize equipment and other objects. That means that all bacteria, viruses, fungi, and spores are inactivated.

Make sure the door of the autoclave is fully closed and the correct cycle has been selected before starting. Wear heat resistant gloves when cracking the autoclave door open after a run. Before removing items, wait 5 minutes for loads containing only dry glassware, and 10 minutes for autoclaved liquid.
